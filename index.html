About the Composer

Ramaprasad KV is a composer working within the Karnataka Sangeeta tradition, and has composed about one hundred compositions in this genre. Many of his compositions have been featured in music and dance concerts. He is a published author with four Kannada books to his credit.

This archive documents an evolving body of work, bringing together notated compositions and audio recordings to help those who would like to learn them. The notations are generally bilingual in English & Kannada.
