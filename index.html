🎶 Original Compositions

This archive presents a collection of original compositions in the Karnataka Sangeeta tradition (also called Carnatic in English), composed by Ramaprasad KV (@Hamsanandi on X). Each entry includes notation and, where available, audio renderings. The compositions may be browsed by type or searched by rāga, tāla, or title, providing an easy way to explore the collection.

About the Composer

Ramaprasad KV(@hamsanandi) is a composer in the Karnataka Sangeeta tradition. He has about 100 compositions to his credit many of which are featured here on this page.

This archive documents an evolving body of work, bringing together notated compositions and audio realizations, and reflecting an ongoing engagement with both traditional frameworks and creative exploration within the idiom.
