🎶 Original Compositions

This archive presents a collection of my original compositions in the Karnataka Sangeeta tradition (also referred to as Carnatic music). Each entry includes notation and, where available, audio renderings. Explore the collection!

About the Composer

Ramaprasad KV is a composer in the Karnataka Sangeeta tradition, with over 100 original compositions across a range of compositional forms. Many of his compositions have been performed on stage and also choreographed in dance concerts. He is also a published author with 4 Kannada books to his credit.

This archive documents an evolving body of work, bringing together notated compositions and audio renditions to help those who would like to learn these compositions.
